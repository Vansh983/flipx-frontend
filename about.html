We are the best Sneaker platform on the internet

FlipX is India’s First Streetwear reselling platform . Our goals are to provide authentic streetwear to customers without any hassle and . We are a platform which allows auctioning of streetwear so multiple buyers and sellers can have the option for a reasonable deal.

 

 

 

The marketplace takes place over the internet where buyers can place bids and sellers can place asks via the “FlipX” platform, further the buyers have an option to buy directly from FlipX in which the product has a fixed price ,all time availability and 100% Verified Items.

We are the clutch for the sneakerheads India was missing. FlipX provides both the buyer and the seller with the usable data like current market value, number of items sold, loss or gain on items, future release dates, transparent sales charts. This data can be used to ensure you never overpay or undersell.

 

 

We have sneaker verification experts which verifies all the sneakers sold from our platform. When a deal is finalised between the seller and a buyer the seller ships the item to the FlipX warehouse , where the item is verified by the experts then shipped to the buyer.
